I'm a PhD Student in Artificial Intelligence for Society at the Universities of Messina and Pisa (National PhD Programme in AI, MUR), affiliated with the Future Computing Research Laboratory (FCRLab) at UniME.

I hold an MSc in Data Science (110/110 cum laude) and a BSc in Computer Science (108/110), both from the University of Messina. My MSc thesis introduced a real-time digital regulatory approval framework for Agile Pharmaceutical Manufacturing using AI and Blockchain.

My research lives at the crossroads of machine learning optimization, blockchain-based data certification, and trustworthy AI. I'm currently extending this work toward Orbital Federated Learning — applying distributed learning paradigms to satellite and space-edge nodes to enable verifiable AI in constrained orbital environments.

In 2025 this work was funded by the European project Neurokit2E. I also collaborate with Circular Protocol (Boston) on blockchain certification for decentralised manufacturing workflows.

// Research areas

What I Work On

🧠

ML Model Compression

Pruning, quantization (QaaS), and KAN networks to reduce the computational footprint of neural networks while preserving predictive accuracy for edge and IoT deployment.

🌐

Cloud-Edge Continuum

Adaptive inference, FaaS architectures built on OpenFaaS + Kubernetes, and context-aware model distribution across cloud, edge, and IoT layers.

🛸

Orbital Federated Learning

Extending federated learning paradigms to satellite and space-edge nodes — enabling privacy-preserving, energy-efficient, and verifiable AI in orbital environments.

⛓️

Blockchain for AI Certification

Cryptographically verifiable AI pipelines, provenance tracking and immutable data certification. Every model inference — auditable and ethically accountable.

📈

AIOps & Telemetry Forecasting

Temporal Transformer models pretrained on Azure VM data and fine-tuned on Alibaba container workloads for real-time CPU/GPU/memory prediction and anomaly detection.

🏭

Digital Manufacturing

Decentralized architectures for pharmaceutical certification and industrial data traceability — from smart contract design to compliance-ready data exchange in regulated environments.
